Story of Rohit Bansal

Rohit Bansal



Bansal was born in Malout, Punjab India.[4] He completed his school education at Delhi Public School (DPS) New Delhi and got his bachelor's degree in engineering from Indian Institute of Technology New Delhi.His father was a grain merchant and mother a homemaker.[11] Bansal is married to Parul, who is an architect. He also has 3 children, Vyas Bansal, Ira Bansal and Kunal Bansal. He has 2 elder sisters, Chinkle Bansal and Twinkle Bansal
